# Application: [Wysa]
- Most important difference between pipelines: [The manual pipeline was more specific and clearly connected to the review groups, while the automated pipeline was faster but more general. The hybrid pipeline improved the automated output and made it more clear.]

- Most useful pipeline: [The hybrid pipeline was the most useful because it gave a good balance between speed and quality. It was better than the automated pipeline alone and took less effort than doing everything manually.]

- Most surprising finding: [The automated requirements looked correct at first, but some of them were too vague when I checked whether they could actually be tested properly.]

- Observed weakness in the automated pipeline: [Some of the automated personas and requirements were too broad and included details that were not strongly supported by the reviews.]
